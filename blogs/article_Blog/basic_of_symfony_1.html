üîπ What You Learn from This Example

Symfony gives you services (LoanService, LoggerInterface, LoanRepo) automatically.

// No messy "new" keyword everywhere

    throw new NotEnoughParamException('user');

üîπ Why This Matters

  • Controller ‚Üí should stay small, call a Service
  • Service ‚Üí does the business logic (your class)
  • Repository ‚Üí handles database work
  • Logger ‚Üí helps debug
  • External Service ‚Üí connects to outside APIs

This separation keeps Symfony apps clean, testable, and easy to grow.
